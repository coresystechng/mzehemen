    Our Focus Areas

    Key initiatives where awareness can drive meaningful change

    Promoting Entrepreneurship

    We aim to provide financial support, training, and mentorship to aspiring Tiv entrepreneurs. This initiative helps foster sustainable businesses and promotes job creation within the community.

    Empowering Women & Youth

    We implement targeted programs and initiatives that empower Tiv women and youth, enhancing their active participation in both economic and social development.

    Education & Healthcare

    Our goal is to collaborate with local stakeholders to improve access to quality education and healthcare services for Tiv communities.

    Preserving Tiv Cultural Heritage

    We support cultural initiatives designed to preserve and promote Tiv traditions, language, and arts, thereby strengthening community identity and pride.

    Recognition Programs

    Our honors system acknowledges individuals who have made significant achievements in public life or have dedicated themselves to supporting Tiv causes.

    Our Core Values

    The principles that guide our awareness efforts

    Community-Driven

    We actively engage with Tiv communities to understand their needs and ensure our initiatives align with their priorities.

    Empowerment

    We foster self-reliance and an entrepreneurial spirit among Tiv individuals, enabling them to become catalysts for change.

    Sustainability

    We implement sustainable solutions that create long-lasting impact and contribute to the long-term prosperity of Tiv communities.

    Cultural Sensitivity

    We respect and promote Tiv cultural values and traditions, ensuring our efforts align with the community's ethos.
