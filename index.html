    Who We Are

    Welcome to Mzehemen U Tiv, a vibrant non-profit organization dedicated to improving the social and economic situation in Tiv land, Benue State, Nigeria. We aim to make the Tiv nation stronger by promoting our ambitions, celebrating our culture, and honoring those who impact society in a positive way. Established in 2019 by Professor Kohol Iornem, we wish to create a vibrant and sustainable Tiv community where every person is able to access the resources and opportunities they require to realize their full potential. Mzehemen U Tiv is incorporated in Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission and in the United Kingdom with Companies House. We lead in serving the community, innovating, and keeping our culture alive. We also recognize and thank sons and daughters of Tiv who are making our society more beautiful. Join us as we work to serve and inspire the Tiv people!

    Our Interests


    We are particularly interested in the following areas.
    Promoting Entrepreneurship and Innovation.

    We aim to provide financial support, training, and mentorship to aspiring Tiv entrepreneurs.

    Empowering Women and Youth.

    We plan to implement targeted programs and initiatives that empower Tiv women and youth.

    Enhancing Access to Education and Healthcare.

    Our goal is to collaborate with local stakeholders to improve access to healthcare services for Tiv communities.

    Mzehemen Awards


    We invite you to support Mzehemen U Tiv in its noble mission to foster a thriving Tiv nation.

    The Integrity Award

    Recognizes an individual who upholds the highest ethical standards and is dedicated to serving others through transparency and accountability.

    The Young Achiever Award

    Recognises young people's achievements. We invest in their future capabilities as young activists by offering cash prizes, access to project awards, money-can't-buy opportunities, and mentoring.

    The Public Service Award

    Recognises distinguished service to country, state, community, or public, with respect to local custom and considering the different cultural perceptions of public service, through excellence in leadership, service, or humanitarianism.
