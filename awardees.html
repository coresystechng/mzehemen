    WOMAN OF THE YEAR AWARD

    Presented to

    Gbadamosi Oluwatosin Victoria
    (Terkumaiwuese)

    Gbadamosi Oluwatosin Victoria, also known as Terkumaiwuese, is from Oyo State. She earned her Higher National Diploma in Estate Management and Valuation.

    During her National Youth Service Corps (NYSC), she served at Mbagwa Community Secondary School in Lessel, Benue State, where she initiated a project to renovate the schoolâ€™s library.

    Her passion for Tiv culture was sparked by the distinctiveness of the Tiv language, which intrigued her and motivated her to learn it. She was also impressed by the hospitality of the Tiv people. She said, "The people are loving and accommodating." This experience inspired her to explore more about the Tiv community and its traditions.

    Victoria has actively promoted the Tiv culture on her social media accounts, where she shares videos of herself singing in Tiv, speaking the language, and dancing to Tiv songs.

    In recognition of her outstanding contributions to the Benue community as a National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) member, as well as her deep love and passion for Tiv culture, the Board of Trustees is proud to present her with the Woman of the Year Award.

    Given this 12th day of June 2025.

    Professor Kohol Iornem
    Founder & Executive Director
    Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation
