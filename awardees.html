    WOMAN OF THE YEAR AWARD

    Presented to

    Gbadamosi Oluwatosin Victoria
    (Terkumaiwuese)

    Gbadamosi Oluwatosin Victoria, also known as Terkumaiwuese, is from Oyo State. She earned her Higher National Diploma in Estate Management and Valuation.During her National Youth Service Corps (NYSC), she served at Mbagwa Community Secondary School in Lessel, Benue State, where she initiated a project to renovate the school’s library. Her passion for Tiv culture was sparked by the distinctiveness of the Tiv language, which intrigued her and motivated her to learn it. She was also impressed by the hospitality of the Tiv people. She said, "The people are loving and accommodating." This experience inspired her to explore more about the Tiv community and its traditions.

    Victoria has actively promoted the Tiv culture on her social media accounts, where she shares videos of herself singing in Tiv, speaking the language, and dancing to Tiv songs. In recognition of her outstanding contributions to the Benue community as a National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) member, as well as her deep love and passion for Tiv culture, the Board of Trustees is proud to present her with the Woman of the Year Award.

    Given this 12th day of June 2025.

    Professor Kohol Iornem
    Founder & Executive Director
    Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation

    COMMUNITY IMPACT AWARD

    Presented to

    Prof. David Iornem

    The Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation (MUTF) has announced that Prof. David Iornem, President of the New Idea Commonwealth Education Group, will be honoured with the Community Impact Award on Friday, August 15, in Gboko, Benue State.

    The award, which coincides with his 76th birthday, recognises individuals whose character, service, and contributions have created lasting positive impact within the Tiv community and beyond. According to MUTF’s Founder and Executive Director, Prof. Kohol Iornem, the honour acknowledges Prof. Iornem’s exceptional commitment to expanding access to free, quality education in Benue State and at his university in the Benin Republic.

    An internationally recognised management consultant, academic, and author, Prof. Iornem has held extensive professional, academic, and public service roles in Nigeria and abroad. His career spans senior positions in multinational companies, legislative service as a senator, leadership roles in political and professional bodies, and teaching appointments across universities in Africa, Europe, and the Americas.

    Given this 15th day of August 2025.

    Professor Kohol Iornem
    Founder & Executive Director
    Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation

    PUBLIC SERVICE AWARD

    Presented to

    Prof. Shima Gyoh, OFR

    Professor Shima Kaimon Gyoh has been honoured with The Public Service Award in recognition of his lifelong service to Nigeria through exemplary leadership, professionalism, and humanitarian commitment to public health. A pioneering surgeon, medical educator, and the first Tiv medical doctor, he has dedicated more than five decades to advancing healthcare delivery, medical education, and public service. Trained at Durham University and a Fellow of both the Royal College of Surgeons (England) and the West African College of Surgeons, Professor Gyoh has consistently combined clinical excellence with visionary national service.

    During his career, Professor Gyoh played a significant role in Nigeria’s public health reforms, particularly during his service at the Federal Ministry of Health alongside the late Dr. Olikoye Ransome-Kuti, where he contributed to notable improvements in national health indicators.

    He also provided transformational leadership at the Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria, strengthening professional regulation and healthcare standards, while mentoring generations of medical professionals as a consultant surgeon and educator.

    Given this 21st day of December 2025.

    Professor Kohol Iornem
    Founder & Executive Director
    Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation
