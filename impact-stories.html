    The Gboko Women's Cooperative

    Empowering women entrepreneurs through financial support and training

    In 2023, Mzehemen U Tiv launched a community-based initiative to support women entrepreneurs in Gboko. Through our financial assistance program and entrepreneurship training, we helped establish the Gboko Women's Cooperative, which now has over 50 active members running successful small businesses.

    The cooperative has not only improved the economic status of these women but has also created employment opportunities for youth in the community. Members of the cooperative have reported an average 60% increase in household income since joining the program.
