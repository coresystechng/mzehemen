    • Our Mission & Vision

    Guiding principles that drive our organization forward

    Our Vision

    We envision a unified Tiv nation that is both resourceful and self-sufficient, enabling us to gain recognition on both national and global levels. This unity will empower us to flourish and create a meaningful impact.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to advance the development of the Tiv nation by supporting Tiv causes, promoting Tiv culture and traditions, and recognizing the achievements of Tiv people who contribute positively to society.

    Our Core Values

    The principles that guide our actions and decisions

    Community

    We believe in the power of community and collective action to create positive change for the Tiv people.

    Cultural Preservation

    We are committed to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Tiv people.

    Integrity

    We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our endeavors.

    Innovation

    We embrace creative solutions and new ideas to address the challenges facing our community.

    Excellence

    We strive for the highest standards in all our programs and initiatives.

    Collaboration

    We believe in working together with all stakeholders to achieve our common goals.

    Our Strategic Objectives

    How we're working to achieve our mission and vision

    Cultural Preservation

    Documenting, preserving, and promoting Tiv cultural heritage, traditions, and language for future generations.

    Community Development

    Implementing programs that address social, economic, and educational needs in Tiv communities.

    Recognition & Awards

    Celebrating and recognizing Tiv individuals who have made significant contributions to society.

    Advocacy & Representation

    Amplifying Tiv voices and advocating for issues that affect the Tiv nation at local, national, and international levels.
