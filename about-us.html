    Our Story

    Welcome to Mzehemen U Tiv, a dynamic non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the social and economic landscape of Tiv land in Benue State, Nigeria.

    Our mission is to strengthen the Tiv nation by supporting our causes, celebrating our culture, and honoring those who positively contribute to society. Founded in 2019 by the visionary Professor Kohol Iornem, we aim to create a thriving and sustainable Tiv community where every individual and group can access the resources and opportunities necessary to reach their full potential.

    Mzehemen U Tiv is registered in Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission and in the United Kingdom with Companies House. We are at the forefront of community empowerment, innovation, and cultural preservation, while also recognizing and appreciating Tiv sons and daughters who make meaningful contributions to improve our society.

    Our Mission, Vision & Values

    Guiding principles that drive our organization forward

    Our Mission

    To strengthen the Tiv nation by supporting our causes, celebrating our culture, and honoring those who positively contribute to society.

    Our Vision

    A thriving and sustainable Tiv community where every individual and group can access the resources and opportunities necessary to reach their full potential.

    Our Values

    Community, Cultural Preservation, Integrity, Innovation, Excellence, Collaboration, and Respect for our heritage and people.

    Our Strategic Objectives

    How we're working to achieve our mission and vision

    Cultural Preservation

    Documenting, preserving, and promoting Tiv cultural heritage, traditions, and language for future generations.

    Community Development

    Implementing programs that address social, economic, and educational needs in Tiv communities.

    Recognition & Awards

    Celebrating and recognizing Tiv individuals who have made significant contributions to society.

    Advocacy & Representation

    Amplifying Tiv voices and advocating for issues that affect the Tiv nation at local, national, and international levels.

    Our Cause

    We are committed to improving the social and economic well-being of Tiv communities. We believe that this investment will create a brighter future for generations to come. Our focus is on collaborating with local partners, community leaders, and individuals to implement sustainable and impactful initiatives that effectively address the unique needs and aspirations of the Tiv people.

    Our Focus Areas

    We are particularly interested in the following areas

    Promoting Entrepreneurship and Innovation

    We aim to provide financial support, training, and mentorship to aspiring Tiv entrepreneurs. This initiative will help foster sustainable businesses and promote job creation within the community.

    Empowering Women and Youth

    We plan to implement targeted programs and initiatives that empower Tiv women and youth, enhancing their active participation in both economic and social development.

    Enhancing Access to Education and Healthcare

    Our goal is to collaborate with local stakeholders to improve access to quality education and healthcare services for Tiv communities.

    Preserving and Promoting Tiv Cultural Heritage

    We support cultural initiatives designed to preserve and promote Tiv traditions, language, and arts, thereby strengthening community identity and pride.

    Recognizing and Celebrating Outstanding Tiv Individuals

    Our honors system acknowledges individuals who have made significant achievements in public life or have dedicated themselves to supporting Tiv causes. These individuals should have positively impacted others' lives.

    Sustainable Community Development

    We focus on initiatives that promote sustainable development in Tiv communities, including infrastructure improvements, agricultural advancements, and environmental conservation efforts that benefit present and future generations.
