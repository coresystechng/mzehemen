    Our Story

    Welcome to Mzehemen U Tiv, a dynamic non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the social and economic landscape of Tiv land in Benue State, Nigeria.

    Our mission is to strengthen the Tiv nation by supporting our causes, celebrating our culture, and honoring those who positively contribute to society. Founded in 2019 by the visionary Professor Kohol Iornem, we aim to create a thriving and sustainable Tiv community where every individual and group can access the resources and opportunities necessary to reach their full potential.

    Mzehemen U Tiv is registered in Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission and in the United Kingdom with Companies House. We are at the forefront of community empowerment, innovation, and cultural preservation, while also recognizing and appreciating Tiv sons and daughters who make meaningful contributions to improve our society.

    Our Journey

    The growth and evolution of Mzehemen U Tiv since 2019

    2019

    Founded by Professor Kohol Iornem with a vision to strengthen and uplift the Tiv nation.

    2020

    Official registration with the Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria and establishment of our first community initiatives.

    2021

    Expanded our reach internationally with registration in the United Kingdom with Companies House.

    2022

    Launched our flagship cultural preservation program and community development initiatives.

    2023

    Established partnerships with key stakeholders to enhance our impact across Tiv land.

    2024-Present

    Continuing to grow our programs and impact, with a focus on sustainable development and cultural heritage preservation.
