    • MUTF Posthumously Confers Community Impact Award on Late Major General Emmanuel Tsehemba Nienge

    The Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation (MUTF) has posthumously conferred the Community Impact Award upon Late Major General Emmanuel Tsehemba Nienge, recognising his unwavering dedication, exemplary leadership, and profound contributions to the upliftment and unity of the Tiv nation.

    The award was received by Barr. (Mrs.) Iveren Blessing Nienge, daughter of the late General, at a ceremony held on August 1, 2025. Along with the award plaque, the family received a cash prize of One Hundred Thousand Naira (N100,000.00) from the Foundation.

    General Nienge's lifelong commitment to public service was marked by integrity, compassion, and a tireless effort to improve the lives of Tiv sons and daughters. Through his efforts in creating employment opportunities and fostering communal harmony, he stood as a beacon of hope and a unifying force within the Tiv community.

    "His remarkable legacy of selfless service and impactful leadership continues to inspire generations, and it is with deep gratitude that we acknowledge the enduring impact of his life's work."

    Professor Kohol Iornem, Founder and Executive Director of Mzehemen U Tiv Foundation, stated that the award recognises General Nienge's significant contributions to the Tiv community and his role as a distinguished military leader who remained committed to the welfare and progress of his people throughout his service to the nation.

    As a Major General in the Nigerian Army, General Nienge served his country with distinction while maintaining strong connections to his Tiv heritage. His efforts in creating employment opportunities provided livelihoods for many Tiv families, and his work in fostering communal harmony strengthened the bonds within the Tiv community.

    The posthumous award serves as a testament to the lasting impact of General Nienge's work and ensures that his contributions are remembered and celebrated by future generations. His legacy continues to inspire others to serve their communities with the same dedication, integrity, and compassion that he demonstrated throughout his life.

    MUTF is a non-profit organisation that aims to improve the social and economic situation of Tiv land in Benue State, Nigeria. Its mission is to empower the Tiv people, support cultural preservation, and encourage community development.

    Registered in both Nigeria and the UK, MUTF takes the lead initiatives in innovation, empowerment, and celebrating Tiv culture while recognising individuals who make positive contributions to society, including those whose impact continues to resonate even after they have passed.
